Initial setup.
------------------------
FAN1    : case fan
FAN0    : part cooling fan
E0-stop : filament runout 3 pin
Z-probe : GND / +5V / PA1 connected (with PA1 connected to center yellow wire). GND and PC14 not connected.  
Z-stop  : other 2 bl touch connector (black wire to GND and grey wire to PC2). 
THB : thermistor bed
TH0 : thermistor hotend

-> extruder fan connected directly to power +24V.
